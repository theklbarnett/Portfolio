Background

During my undergraduate studies, I pursued a degree in Chemical Engineering from the University of Washington. Upon entering into my freshman year in 2015, I had already accumulated a majority of credits for the Chemistry program but decided to apply to the Chemical Engineering program because of the problem solving nature of engineering majors. However towards the end of my freshman year I took an intro class on Java and fell in love with programming. Only having 2 years left in my degree, I refused to give up on something I had already started. Especially with the extremely competitive nature of the University's STEM admittance and my acceptance into the Chemical Engineering program.

Fast forward to my last quarter before graduation, I decided to take a course on advanced Java data structures and rediscovered my passion for computer science. Since graduation I have independantly honed my skills, and most recently graduated from the "Coding Dojo" coding bootcamp.
